About me

Composer | Producer | Songwriter | Mixing & Mastering Engineer
I'm a Ukrainian composer, producer, and sound engineer with over 5 years of experience in the music industry. My journey began at the age of 13 when I first downloaded a demo version of FL Studio, and I've been hooked ever since. Since then, I've worked extensively with artists to create music that resonates with listeners, handling everything from composition and songwriting to mixing and mastering.
While my primary focus has been collaborating with musicians and producing tracks for direct audiences, I’ve always had a deep passion for exploring new creative avenues—especially in game development. I’m eager to apply my skills to create immersive soundscapes and dynamic audio experiences that bring virtual worlds to life.
With a solid foundation in both artistic expression and technical expertise, I’m ready to take on new challenges, from scoring video games to designing soundtracks that elevate the player experience. Let's make something extraordinary together.
